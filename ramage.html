Athlete and Volunteer

January 8, 1968 - April 29, 2019

Bradley Ramage was an inspirational member of the Pittsburgh parasport community and a world-class athlete in his own right. A five-time Paralympic medalist, including two gold medals in track and field competitions, Ramage continued competing throughout the rest of his life in hand-cycling and with the Pittsburgh Steelwheelers. Ramage was also known for his work with Sunrise Medical, which specializes in power wheelchairs and mobility technology, and as an empowering figure to young hockey players in the Pennsylvania area.
