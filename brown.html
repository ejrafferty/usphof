Organizer

Tom Brown is a recreational therapist for the Department of Veterans Affairs who founded the National Veterans Wheelchair Games in 1981. Under his direction, the wheelchair games grew in size and impact as thousands of veterans have participated in the games over the past 40 years. Brown, a double amputee himself due to a congenital birth defect, wanted to expose people with disabilities to the possibilities of sports and athletics even if they would not compete at the elite paralympic level.
