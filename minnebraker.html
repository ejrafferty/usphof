Contributor

Jeff Minnebraker is one of the integral figures in the very invention of wheelchair tennis as a sport and was key in the expansion of the game to a global audience. Minnebraker, an L1 paraplegic himself, was unhappy with the ability of the standard hospital wheelchair to perform in faster athletic environments. He created his own lightweight wheelchair for use in more intense competition, and to allow him and his friends to play the sport they love at a more advanced level.
