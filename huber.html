Athlete and Contributor

March 4, 1951 - June 16, 2009

Ken Huber was a widely respected parasport athlete, advocate, and organizer. A Navy veteran, Huber continued to serve his community in many leadership roles including as president of Paralyzed Veterans of America from 1996-98. Huber was a long-time participant in the National Veterans Wheelchair Games and was inducted into the Michigan Disabled Sports Hall of Fame for his impact within the community.
