US Parasport Hall of Fame

About the Hall

The US Parasport Hall of Fame was launched in 2022 to recognize the great achievements and dedication to parasport of influential persons, highlight the importance of their contributions to the community, and inspire others to follow in their footsteps. The virtual information hub represents a continued appreciation for the elite athletes, coaches, volunteers, organizers, scientists, and other contributors who have committed their time and passion across the world of parasports.

The USPHOF's honorees feature Collectible Cards including artistic renditions of numerous impactful figures within the parasport community. The artwork was created by Zachary Roy.
